## 1. Scope and Applicability

This document governs all voluntary and involuntary changes and cancellations to bookings made on SkyItalia Airlines. It applies to tickets issued directly by SkyItalia (via skyitalia.com, mobile app, contact centre, or ticket offices) as well as tickets purchased through authorised travel agents and online travel agencies (OTAs) that carry the SkyItalia (AZ) designator as the marketing carrier.

For tickets issued on other carriers' ticket stock (e.g., interline or codeshare partners where the other carrier is the marketing carrier), the issuing carrier's change and cancellation policy prevails. Passengers holding such tickets should contact the issuing carrier directly. SkyItalia customer service agents may not process changes or cancellations on tickets where SkyItalia is not the marketing carrier, except for involuntary reprotection under Irregular Operations (IROP) procedures.

This policy does not apply to charter flights, wet-lease operations where SkyItalia acts solely as the operating carrier, or special government-contracted flights. Separate policies exist for cargo shipments and unaccompanied baggage consignments.

### 1.1 Fare Type Definitions

SkyItalia offers five distinct fare types across its route network. Each fare type carries specific change and cancellation entitlements as described in this document:

| Fare Type | Fare Code | Cabin | Primary Characteristics |
|-----------|-----------|-------|------------------------|
| Economy Light | EL | Economy | Lowest fare, limited flexibility, no checked baggage included |
| Economy Standard | ES | Economy | Mid-range fare, moderate flexibility, 1 checked bag included |
| Economy Flex | EF | Economy | Full flexibility, 1 checked bag included, priority boarding |
| Premium Economy | PE | Premium Economy | Enhanced cabin, full flexibility, 2 checked bags, lounge voucher |
| Business | BZ | Business | Front cabin, maximum flexibility, 2 checked bags (32 kg each), lounge access |

The fare type is determined at the time of booking and is printed on the electronic ticket receipt (e-ticket). Fare type downgrades are not permitted; a passenger wishing to move to a lower fare type must cancel the existing booking (subject to the applicable cancellation policy) and create a new booking at the desired fare.

### 1.2 General Processing Information

All change and cancellation requests can be submitted through the following channels:

- **Online:** skyitalia.com > Manage My Booking
- **Mobile App:** SkyItalia app > My Trips
- **Contact Centre:** +39 02 8800 7700 (Italy), +44 20 7946 0958 (UK), +33 1 70 36 39 50 (France), +34 91 787 0120 (Spain)
- **Airport Ticket Office:** Available at all SkyItalia stations during operating hours
- **Travel Agent:** Through the original booking agent's GDS terminal (Amadeus, Sabre, or Travelport)

Processing times vary by channel. Online and app changes are processed immediately. Contact centre requests are processed within the call. Airport ticket office and travel agent requests may take up to 4 hours to reflect in the reservation system. During peak travel periods (Christmas, Easter, August), contact centre wait times may exceed 45 minutes; online self-service is strongly recommended.

## 2. Voluntary Change Policy by Fare Type

A voluntary change is any modification to a booking initiated by the passenger (or their authorised representative) that is not the result of a SkyItalia schedule change, cancellation, or irregular operation.

### 2.1 Types of Changes

SkyItalia classifies voluntary changes into the following categories:

1. **Date/Time Change** — Moving the booking to a different date or a different departure time on the same date.
2. **Route Change** — Changing the origin and/or destination airport (where permitted by fare rules).
3. **Name Change** — Modifying the passenger name on the booking.
4. **Class/Cabin Change** — Moving to a higher cabin class (upgrade). Downgrades are treated as cancellation + rebooking.
5. **Airport Change (Same City)** — Switching between airports serving the same city (e.g., MXP and LIN in Milan, or FCO and CIA in Rome).

### 2.2 Comprehensive Change Fee Matrix

The following table shows the applicable fees for each type of change, broken down by fare type. All fees are in EUR and are charged per passenger, per direction. Fare differences are always additional and apply on top of the change fee.

| Change Type | Economy Light | Economy Standard | Economy Flex | Premium Economy | Business |
|------------|:------------:|:----------------:|:------------:|:---------------:|:--------:|
| Date/Time Change | EUR 75 + fare diff | EUR 50 + fare diff | Free (fare diff may apply) | Free (no fare diff within same bucket) | Free (no fare diff on same-day) |
| Route Change | Not permitted | EUR 75 + fare diff | Free (fare diff may apply) | Free (fare diff may apply) | Free (fare diff may apply) |
| Name Change | Not permitted | EUR 50 | EUR 25 | Free | Free |
| Spelling Correction | Free (up to 3 chars) | Free | Free | Free | Free |
| Same-City Airport Change | EUR 75 | EUR 50 | Free | Free | Free |
| Cabin Upgrade | See Section 8 | See Section 8 | See Section 8 | See Section 8 | N/A |

**Notes on fare differences:**
- A fare difference is charged when the new flight has a higher booking class fare than the original. If the new fare is lower, no refund of the fare difference is provided for Economy Light and Economy Standard. Economy Flex, Premium Economy, and Business passengers receive the difference as travel credit (not cash refund).
- Fare differences are calculated based on the fare available at the time of the change, not the original booking date.
- Taxes and surcharges may also differ and will be adjusted accordingly.

### 2.3 Economy Light — Detailed Change Rules

Economy Light is SkyItalia's most restrictive fare type, designed for price-sensitive travellers with fixed plans.

**Date/Time Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 75** per passenger, per direction, plus any applicable fare difference.
- The change must be requested at least **3 hours before the scheduled departure time** of the original flight.
- Changes requested within 3 hours of departure are not permitted; the passenger must purchase a new ticket.
- A maximum of **2 date/time changes** are permitted per booking. After the second change, no further changes are allowed.
- Changes are permitted only within the same route. Cross-route changes require cancellation and rebooking.
- The new travel date must be within **90 days** of the original departure date.

**Route Change:**
- **Not permitted.** Passengers wishing to change their route must cancel the booking (subject to the Economy Light cancellation policy, which is non-refundable) and purchase a new ticket.

**Name Change:**
- **Not permitted.** Economy Light tickets are non-transferable. Minor spelling corrections (up to 3 characters) can be made free of charge by contacting the SkyItalia contact centre or at the airport ticket office. This does not include changes to the legal identity of the passenger.

**Same-City Airport Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 75** per passenger, per direction.
- Available only where SkyItalia operates flights from both airports in the same city (e.g., Milan MXP/LIN, Rome FCO/CIA).
- Subject to seat availability on the desired flight from the alternate airport.

### 2.4 Economy Standard — Detailed Change Rules

Economy Standard offers moderate flexibility and is the most popular fare type on SkyItalia's network.

**Date/Time Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 50** per passenger, per direction, plus any applicable fare difference.
- The change must be requested at least **2 hours before the scheduled departure time** of the original flight.
- A maximum of **3 date/time changes** are permitted per booking.
- The new travel date must be within **180 days** of the original departure date.

**Route Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 75** per passenger, per direction, plus any applicable fare difference.
- The change must be requested at least **2 hours before departure**.
- Route changes are permitted only within the same geographical region (domestic Italy, intra-EU, or intercontinental). Cross-region route changes are not permitted and require cancellation and rebooking.

**Name Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 50** per passenger.
- Name changes are permitted up to **48 hours before departure**.
- Only one name change is allowed per booking. This covers legitimate situations such as legal name changes due to marriage, court orders, or passport reissuance. It does not constitute a ticket transfer; the original passenger and the new name must refer to the same individual. SkyItalia reserves the right to request supporting documentation (marriage certificate, deed poll, etc.).
- **Spelling corrections** (up to 3 characters) are processed **free of charge** at any time before departure.

**Same-City Airport Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 50** per passenger, per direction.
- Available subject to SkyItalia operating the same route from the alternate airport.

### 2.5 Economy Flex — Detailed Change Rules

Economy Flex provides full flexibility within the Economy cabin and is designed for business travellers and passengers who anticipate schedule uncertainty.

**Date/Time Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Fare difference may apply if the new flight is in a higher booking class. If the new fare is lower, the difference is issued as travel credit (valid 12 months).
- Changes can be made up to **1 hour before the scheduled departure time**.
- Unlimited changes are permitted.
- The new travel date must be within **365 days** of the original booking date.

**Route Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Fare difference may apply based on the new route's pricing.
- Changes can be made up to **1 hour before departure**.
- Route changes within the same geographical region are processed immediately. Cross-region route changes may require manual processing (allow up to 4 hours).

**Name Change:**
- Fee: **EUR 25** per passenger.
- Permitted up to **24 hours before departure**.
- Same restrictions as Economy Standard regarding identity verification.
- **Spelling corrections** are **free of charge**.

**Same-City Airport Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Subject to availability.

### 2.6 Premium Economy — Detailed Change Rules

Premium Economy passengers enjoy the enhanced cabin experience with maximum booking flexibility.

**Date/Time Change:**
- Fee: **Free.** No fare difference is charged if the change is within the same fare bucket (PE booking class).
- If the new flight is only available in a higher fare bucket, a fare difference may apply.
- Changes can be made up to **1 hour before the scheduled departure time**.
- Unlimited changes are permitted.

**Route Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Fare difference may apply for routes with significantly different base fares.
- Changes can be made up to **1 hour before departure**.

**Name Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Permitted up to **24 hours before departure**.
- Supporting documentation may be requested.
- Spelling corrections are free of charge.

**Same-City Airport Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Priority handling at the new departure airport.

### 2.7 Business Class — Detailed Change Rules

Business Class offers the most comprehensive flexibility and priority service across all booking change scenarios.

**Date/Time Change:**
- Fee: **Free.** No fare difference is charged for same-day changes regardless of booking class.
- For changes to a different date, fare differences may apply only if the new date falls within a blackout period (e.g., major holidays).
- Changes can be made up to **departure time** — even at the gate.
- Unlimited changes are permitted.

**Route Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Fare difference may apply for different routes if the base fare is significantly higher.
- Changes can be made up to departure time.

**Name Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Permitted up to **12 hours before departure**.
- Spelling corrections are free of charge.

**Priority Rebooking:**
- Business Class passengers automatically receive priority for the next available flight in the event of missed connections, delays, or voluntary same-day changes.
- Priority rebooking places Business Class passengers ahead of Economy passengers on the standby list.

**Same-City Airport Change:**
- Fee: **Free.**
- Complimentary ground transfer between airports may be arranged for Business Class passengers upon request (subject to availability and timing; typically offered when the schedule change results from SkyItalia operational decisions).

## 3. Name Change Policy — Detailed Provisions

### 3.1 Spelling Corrections vs. Name Changes

SkyItalia distinguishes between a **spelling correction** and a **name change**:

- **Spelling Correction:** A minor adjustment of up to **3 characters** in the passenger's name (e.g., "Giovani" corrected to "Giovanni", or "Smth" corrected to "Smith"). Spelling corrections are free of charge on all fare types and can be made at any time before departure.
- **Name Change:** Any modification that alters more than 3 characters, changes the first name or surname substantially, or results in a different legal identity on the booking. Name change fees and restrictions apply per fare type (see Section 2).

### 3.2 Non-Transferability

SkyItalia tickets are **non-transferable**. A name change is not intended to enable one person to give or sell their ticket to another person. The name change policy exists solely to accommodate:
- Legal name changes (marriage, divorce, court order)
- Corrections where the booking was made with a significantly misspelled name
- Passport reissuance resulting in a name format change

SkyItalia reserves the right to deny a name change request if it appears to constitute a ticket transfer. If a name change request is denied, the passenger's sole remedy is to cancel the booking (subject to fare rules) and rebook under the correct name.

### 3.3 Documentation Requirements for Name Changes

For name changes exceeding a spelling correction, the following documentation may be required:
- Valid government-issued photo ID showing the new name
- Supporting legal document (marriage certificate, court order, or deed poll)
- Original booking confirmation

Documentation must be submitted via email to namechange@skyitalia.com or presented at the airport ticket office. Processing time is up to **48 hours** from receipt of complete documentation. Passengers are advised to initiate name change requests well in advance of travel.

### 3.4 Group Bookings and Name Changes

For group bookings (10 or more passengers), name changes follow special rules (see Section 7 — Group Booking Rules).

## 4. Cancellation Policy by Fare Type

### 4.1 Economy Light — Cancellation

- **Non-refundable.** The ticket price is forfeited upon cancellation.
- Airport taxes and government-imposed fees may be refunded upon request. A **processing fee of EUR 25** is deducted from the tax refund amount.
- To request a tax refund, passengers must submit a written request via the SkyItalia website (Manage My Booking > Request Tax Refund) or by email to refunds@skyitalia.com within **12 months** of the original departure date.
- Economy Light passengers may convert the ticket value (minus a EUR 50 conversion fee) to a **SkyItalia travel voucher** valid for 6 months, provided the request is made at least 48 hours before departure. This option is not available after the departure time has passed.

### 4.2 Economy Standard — Cancellation

- **Refundable** with a penalty of **EUR 100** per passenger, per direction.
- Refund amount = ticket price minus EUR 100.
- Airport taxes and government-imposed fees are **fully refundable** with no processing fee.
- Cancellation must be requested before the scheduled departure time. Post-departure cancellations are not eligible for refund (treated as no-show; see Section 5).
- The refund is issued to the original form of payment. See Section 9 for refund processing timelines.
- As an alternative to a cash refund, passengers may opt for a **SkyItalia travel voucher** for the full ticket value (no EUR 100 penalty deducted) valid for **12 months** from the date of cancellation.

### 4.3 Economy Flex — Cancellation

- **Fully refundable**, no penalty.
- Refund is processed within **7 business days** of the cancellation request.
- Cancellation can be requested at any time before departure.
- Post-departure cancellations for unused segments (e.g., the return leg of a round-trip) are also fully refundable.

### 4.4 Premium Economy — Cancellation

- **Fully refundable**, no penalty.
- Refund is processed within **5 business days** of the cancellation request.
- Same provisions as Economy Flex regarding timing and unused segments.

### 4.5 Business Class — Cancellation

- **Fully refundable**, no penalty.
- Refund is processed within **3 business days** of the cancellation request.
- **Travel credit option:** Business Class passengers may opt to convert the refund into a SkyItalia travel credit with a **10% bonus** added to the value. For example, a EUR 500 ticket refunded as travel credit would yield EUR 550 in credit. Travel credit is valid for **18 months** from the date of issue and can be used for any SkyItalia fare type or ancillary purchase.
- Business Class cancellations are processed with priority by the dedicated Business Service team.

|-----------|:-----------:|:-------:|:----------:|:-------------------:|:--------:|
| Economy Light | No | N/A (forfeited) | Yes (minus EUR 25) | Voucher (minus EUR 50, 6 mo) | Before departure |
| Economy Standard | Yes | EUR 100 | Yes (full) | Full value voucher (12 mo) | Before departure |

## 5. No-Show Policy

A no-show occurs when a passenger fails to present at the boarding gate by the gate closure time and does not cancel the booking in advance.

### 5.1 No-Show Rules by Fare Type

**Economy Light / Economy Standard:**
- The ticket is **forfeited**. No refund is provided.
- Any subsequent segments on the same booking (e.g., return flights, onward connections) are automatically cancelled.
- Airport taxes are not refundable in the case of a no-show.
- If the passenger had a connecting itinerary and missed the first segment due to a delay on a preceding SkyItalia flight, the no-show policy does not apply; the passenger is reprotected under Irregular Operations procedures.

**Economy Flex:**
- The ticket value is converted to a **SkyItalia travel credit** valid for **6 months** from the date of the no-show.
- Subsequent segments on the same booking remain active. The passenger can check in for later segments without penalty.
- The travel credit can be used for any SkyItalia booking (flights, ancillaries, upgrades).

**Premium Economy / Business:**
- The passenger is **automatically rebooked** on the next available SkyItalia flight on the same route at **no additional cost**.
- If no SkyItalia flight is available on the same day, the passenger is offered rebooking on the next day's first available flight or a full refund.
- Subsequent segments on the same booking remain active.
- Premium Economy and Business no-show rebooking is handled by the Priority Service desk. Passengers can call the dedicated phone line or present at the SkyItalia transfer desk at the airport.

### 5.2 Partial No-Show (Multi-Segment Itineraries)

For bookings containing multiple flight segments (e.g., outbound + return, or multi-city itineraries):
- If the passenger no-shows on the first segment, all subsequent segments are automatically cancelled for Economy Light and Economy Standard.
- Economy Flex passengers retain all subsequent segments; only the missed segment is converted to travel credit.
- Premium Economy and Business passengers retain all subsequent segments; the missed segment is handled per Section 5.1.

### 5.3 No-Show vs. Late Arrival

A passenger who arrives at the gate after closure but before the aircraft door is closed may be accommodated at the discretion of the gate agent. This is not guaranteed and depends on operational factors (boarding bridge status, baggage loading, departure slot). If accommodated, no no-show penalty applies. SkyItalia recommends arriving at the gate at least 30 minutes before departure for domestic flights and 45 minutes for international flights.

## 6. 24-Hour Free Cancellation Rule

### 6.1 Eligibility

All SkyItalia bookings can be cancelled **free of charge within 24 hours of purchase**, regardless of fare type. This right is provided in accordance with SkyItalia's customer commitment and applies to all booking channels (online, app, contact centre, travel agent, airport ticket office).

### 6.2 Conditions

- The flight must be **at least 7 days** in the future at the time of booking. Flights departing within 7 days of purchase are not eligible for the 24-hour free cancellation.
- The 24-hour window is calculated from the exact time of booking confirmation (timestamp on the confirmation email), not from the date of booking.
- The cancellation must be initiated within the 24-hour window. It does not need to be fully processed within that window (e.g., if a contact centre call is initiated at hour 23 and the processing takes 30 minutes, the cancellation is still valid).

### 6.3 Refund Under the 24-Hour Rule

- The refund is **automatic** and issued to the original payment method.
- No cancellation fee, penalty, or processing fee is charged.
- Refunds under this rule are processed within **3-5 business days** regardless of fare type.
- Ancillary purchases (seat selection, extra baggage, priority boarding) made as part of the same transaction are also fully refunded.

### 6.4 Limitations

- This rule applies only to new bookings. It does not apply to changes made to existing bookings.
- Bookings made as part of a package tour (flight + hotel) may be subject to the tour operator's cancellation policy instead.
- Group bookings (10+ passengers) are excluded from the 24-hour free cancellation rule. Group cancellation rules apply (see Section 7).
- Award tickets (booked using SkyMiles points) are covered by the 24-hour rule, but the points refund processing time is 48 hours (see Section 10).

## 7. Group Booking Rules

### 7.1 Definition

A group booking is defined as a reservation for **10 or more passengers** travelling together on the same flight and itinerary. Group bookings are handled by the SkyItalia Group Desk and are subject to special terms that differ from individual bookings.

### 7.2 Group Change Policy

- **Date/Time Changes:** Group date changes must be submitted at least **14 days before departure**. A fee of **EUR 30 per passenger** applies for all fare types (overriding individual fare type change fees). Fare differences apply as normal.
- **Route Changes:** Not permitted for groups. The group must cancel and rebook if a route change is needed.
- **Name Changes:** Up to **20% of passenger names** in a group booking may be changed free of charge until **7 days before departure**. Additional name changes beyond 20% are charged at EUR 25 per name. After the 7-day deadline, standard individual name change fees apply.
- **Reduction of Group Size:** If the group size falls below 10 passengers due to cancellations, the booking is reclassified as individual bookings and the remaining passengers are charged the applicable individual fare (which may be higher than the group fare). A reclassification fee of **EUR 50** applies.

### 7.3 Group Cancellation Policy

- **Full Group Cancellation (30+ days before departure):** Refundable minus a **EUR 15 per passenger** administrative fee.
- **Full Group Cancellation (15-29 days before departure):** Refundable minus **EUR 50 per passenger**.
- **Full Group Cancellation (7-14 days before departure):** Refundable minus **50% of the ticket price** per passenger.
- **Full Group Cancellation (fewer than 7 days):** Non-refundable.
- **Individual Cancellation Within a Group:** The 20% free name change allowance can be used to substitute passengers instead of cancelling. If a genuine cancellation is required, the standard fare-type cancellation policy applies to the individual passenger.

### 7.4 Group Booking Modifications — Processing

All group changes and cancellations must be submitted in writing to groups@skyitalia.com or through the Group Desk portal. Phone requests are not accepted for group modifications. Processing time is **3-5 business days** for standard requests and **24 hours** for urgent requests (within 72 hours of departure, subject to a EUR 50 expedite fee).

## 8. Voluntary Upgrade vs. Cabin Class Change

### 8.1 Voluntary Upgrade (Post-Purchase)

Passengers may request a cabin class upgrade after purchasing their ticket. Upgrade pricing is separate from the fare structure and is based on the route and availability.

**Standard Upgrade Prices:**

| From | To | Price (EUR) |
|------|----|:-----------:|
| Economy (any fare) | Premium Economy | EUR 85 |
| Economy (any fare) | Business | EUR 250 |
| Premium Economy | Business | EUR 180 |

- Upgrade prices are per passenger, per direction.
- Upgrades are subject to availability in the target cabin at the time of the request.
- Upgrades can be requested via Manage My Booking, at the airport check-in desk, or through the contact centre.
- Upgrades requested at the gate (last-minute) may carry a surcharge of EUR 25 on top of the standard upgrade price, depending on the route.

### 8.2 Upgrade Using SkyMiles Points

SkyMiles loyalty programme members may use points for upgrades (subject to tier and availability):

| Target Cabin | Points Required |
|-------------|:--------------:|
| Premium Economy | 15,000 points |
| Business | 25,000 points |

- Points upgrades are confirmed 48 hours before departure on a priority basis (Platinum > Gold > Silver > Blue).
- If the upgrade is not confirmed, the points are returned to the member's account.
- Points upgrades and cash upgrades cannot be combined on the same request.

### 8.3 Cabin Class Change vs. Upgrade

A cabin class change (e.g., voluntarily moving from Business to Economy) is treated as a **cancellation and rebooking**, not as an upgrade. The original booking's cancellation policy applies, and a new booking must be made at the current available fare. No "downgrade refund" is provided for voluntary cabin class reductions.

### 8.4 Involuntary Downgrade

If SkyItalia downgrades a passenger due to operational reasons (e.g., aircraft substitution with fewer Business Class seats), compensation is provided under EU Regulation 261/2004, Article 10:

| Route Distance | Refund of Ticket Price |
|---------------|:---------------------:|
| Up to 1,500 km | 30% |
| 1,501 to 3,500 km | 50% |
| Over 3,500 km | 75% |

This refund is processed within 7 business days and is in addition to any other entitlements the passenger may have.

## 9. Refund Processing Timelines

### 9.1 By Payment Method

Refund processing time depends on the original method of payment and the complexity of the refund request.

| Payment Method | Processing Time | Notes |
|---------------|:--------------:|-------|
| Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) | **7-14 business days** | Refund appears on the next billing statement after processing. The exact timeline depends on the issuing bank's processing cycle. |
| Debit Card | **7-14 business days** | Same processing time as credit cards; however, funds may take an additional 2-3 days to appear in the current account. |
| Bank Transfer / Wire | **14-21 business days** | Requires verification of the original payment. International bank transfers may take up to 28 business days due to intermediary banking. |
| PayPal | **5-10 business days** | Refunded to the PayPal account used for the original purchase. |
| SkyMiles Points | **48 hours** | Points are credited back to the member's SkyMiles account. If the booking was partially paid with points and partially with cash, each component is refunded separately via its respective channel. |
| SkyItalia Travel Voucher | **24 hours** | A new voucher is issued for the refund amount. The new voucher inherits the original voucher's expiry date (not extended). |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | **7-14 business days** | Refund is processed to the underlying card linked to the digital wallet. |

### 9.2 Refund Status Tracking

Passengers can track their refund status via:
- **Online:** skyitalia.com > Manage My Booking > Refund Status
- **Contact Centre:** Refund reference number is required (provided in the cancellation confirmation email)
- **Email:** refunds@skyitalia.com (include booking reference and refund reference number)

### 9.3 Disputed or Failed Refunds

If a refund has not been received within the stated processing time, passengers should first check with their bank or card issuer. If the bank confirms no refund has been received, passengers may submit a refund trace request to refunds@skyitalia.com with the following information:
- Booking reference
- Refund reference number
- Date of cancellation
- Last four digits of the payment card (for card payments) or bank account details (for bank transfers)
- Bank statement showing the original charge (optional but helpful)

SkyItalia will investigate and respond within **10 business days** of receiving a refund trace request.

### 9.4 Currency and Exchange Rates

Refunds are issued in the currency of the original purchase. If the original payment was in a non-EUR currency, the refund amount is determined by the exchange rate at the time of the refund processing, which may differ from the exchange rate at the time of purchase. SkyItalia is not responsible for exchange rate fluctuations.

- Economy Light / Standard award tickets: Points are forfeited (same as revenue ticket — no refund).
- Economy Flex: Points converted to a holding account (valid 6 months).
- Premium Economy / Business: Automatic rebooking on next available flight.

## 11. Involuntary Changes (Airline-Initiated)

### 11.1 Schedule Changes

If SkyItalia modifies the flight schedule after a booking has been confirmed, the following rules apply based on the magnitude of the change:

| Schedule Change Magnitude | Passenger Entitlements |
|--------------------------|----------------------|
| Less than 30 minutes | No action required. Passenger is automatically accommodated. |
| 30 minutes to 2 hours | Passenger is notified and may accept or reject the change. If rejected, free rebooking on the nearest alternative SkyItalia flight is offered. |
| **More than 2 hours** | Free rebooking on any available SkyItalia flight on the same route. **Full refund** if no suitable alternative exists. Compensation may apply under EU Regulation 261/2004 (see Compensation Policy document). |
| Flight cancelled | Full refund or rebooking on next available flight (passenger's choice). EU 261/2004 compensation applies unless the cancellation is due to extraordinary circumstances. |

- Passengers are notified of schedule changes via email (primary) and SMS (if a mobile number is on file) at least 14 days before departure where possible.
- If the schedule change results in a connecting flight becoming non-viable (connection time below the minimum connection time at the transit airport), SkyItalia will automatically reprotect the entire itinerary.

### 11.2 Equipment Change (Aircraft Substitution)

When SkyItalia substitutes the aircraft on a route (e.g., replacing an A321neo with a smaller A320neo), the following may occur:
- Seat assignments may change. Passengers are automatically reassigned to equivalent seats where possible.
- If the substitute aircraft has fewer seats in a cabin class, some passengers may be involuntarily downgraded (see Section 8.4).
- If the substitute aircraft has no Premium Economy cabin, Premium Economy passengers are upgraded to Business Class at no cost (if available) or downgraded to Economy with compensation per Article 10 of EU 261/2004.

### 11.3 Irregular Operations (IROP)

Irregular operations include delays exceeding 3 hours at arrival, cancellations, diversions, and missed connections caused by SkyItalia operational issues. Under IROP:
- All passengers are entitled to rebooking on the next available SkyItalia flight regardless of fare type.
- If no SkyItalia flight is available within 24 hours, rebooking on partner airlines may be offered.
- Passengers may opt for a full refund instead of rebooking.
- Right to care (meals, refreshments, hotel accommodation, ground transport) applies per EU 261/2004, Article 9 (see Compensation Policy document for details).

## 12. Force Majeure and Extraordinary Circumstances

### 12.1 Definition

Force majeure events are extraordinary circumstances beyond SkyItalia's control that could not have been avoided even if all reasonable measures had been taken. These include, but are not limited to:

- **Meteorological conditions:** Severe weather events (hurricanes, volcanic ash clouds, extreme snowfall, thunderstorms) that make flight operations unsafe.
- **Air Traffic Control (ATC) restrictions:** Strikes by ATC personnel, ATC system failures, airspace closures due to military activity.
- **Security threats:** Bomb threats, terrorism alerts, airport evacuations, airspace closures following security incidents.
- **Political instability:** Coups, civil unrest, war, or government-mandated travel bans.
- **Pandemics and health emergencies:** Government-mandated quarantines, border closures, flight suspensions ordered by health authorities.
- **Natural disasters:** Earthquakes, tsunamis, floods affecting airport infrastructure.
- **Airport closures:** Runway damage, fire at the terminal, infrastructure failure (e.g., power outage affecting the entire airport).

### 12.2 Passenger Rights During Force Majeure

During force majeure events, SkyItalia's obligations are modified as follows:
- **Rebooking:** Free rebooking is offered on the next available SkyItalia flight once operations resume. No fare difference is charged regardless of fare type.
- **Refund:** Full refund is available if the passenger chooses not to travel. Refund processing follows standard timelines per Section 9.
- **EU 261/2004 Compensation:** Monetary compensation under EU 261/2004 is **not payable** for disruptions caused by extraordinary circumstances. However, the right to care (meals, hotel, etc.) still applies even during force majeure events.
- **Change fees:** All change fees are waived for bookings affected by force majeure events. This waiver applies to all fare types.
- **Extended rebooking window:** Passengers may rebook within **12 months** of the original departure date at no additional cost.

### 12.3 Documentation for Force Majeure Claims

SkyItalia publishes force majeure declarations on its website (skyitalia.com/travel-alerts). When filing a claim, passengers should reference the specific force majeure notice number. No additional documentation from the passenger is typically required for declared force majeure events.

## 13. Same-City Airport Changes

### 13.1 Milan: MXP (Malpensa) and LIN (Linate)

SkyItalia operates flights from both Milan Malpensa (MXP) and Milan Linate (LIN). Passengers may request to change their departure or arrival airport between MXP and LIN under the following conditions:

- The same route must be operated from the requested airport.
- Seat availability must exist on the desired flight.
- Change fees apply per fare type (see Section 2.2).
- The changed booking retains the same fare type and cabin class.
- Checked baggage allowance transfers without change.
- If the airport change results in a significantly different departure time (more than 3 hours), the passenger must also accept the new time.

**Practical Note:** MXP is located approximately 50 km northwest of Milan, while LIN is approximately 7 km east of the city centre. Ground transfer time between the two airports is approximately 60-90 minutes depending on traffic. SkyItalia does not provide complimentary ground transfer for voluntary airport changes. For involuntary airport changes (e.g., SkyItalia moving a flight from one airport to another), complimentary shuttle or taxi service is provided.

### 13.2 Rome: FCO (Fiumicino) and CIA (Ciampino)

SkyItalia primarily operates from Rome Fiumicino (FCO). Limited seasonal services operate from Rome Ciampino (CIA). Airport changes between FCO and CIA follow the same rules as Milan (see above). Note that most SkyItalia routes are only available from FCO; CIA services are primarily operated by subsidiary or wet-lease partners.

### 13.3 Other Cities

For cities served by a single SkyItalia airport (e.g., Paris CDG, London LHR, Barcelona BCN), same-city airport changes to airports not served by SkyItalia are not possible. Passengers requiring travel to/from a different airport in the same city (e.g., London Gatwick instead of Heathrow) must cancel and rebook with a carrier that serves the desired airport.

## 15. Operational Processing Procedures

### 15.1 Change Processing Workflow (Internal Reference)

When a change request is received, the SkyItalia reservation system (SKY-RES) processes it through the following steps:

1. **Fare Rule Validation:** The system checks the fare type to determine if the requested change is permitted and calculates the applicable fee.
2. **Availability Check:** The system verifies that seats are available on the new requested flight/date/route.
3. **Fare Difference Calculation:** If the new itinerary has a different fare, the system calculates the difference. Higher fares result in a collect; lower fares result in a credit (per fare type rules).
4. **Payment Processing:** The change fee and any fare difference are charged to the payment card on file or collected at the point of sale.
5. **Confirmation:** A new e-ticket is issued and the confirmation is sent to the passenger's email address on file. The original ticket is voided.
6. **Ancillary Revalidation:** Any ancillary services (seat selection, extra baggage, meals) are revalidated for the new flight. If the ancillary is not available on the new flight, a refund is issued for that ancillary.

### 15.2 Cancellation Processing Workflow (Internal Reference)

1. **Eligibility Check:** The system validates the fare type and confirms whether a refund is applicable.
2. **Refund Calculation:** For refundable tickets, the system calculates the refund amount (ticket price minus penalty, if any). For non-refundable tickets, only the tax component is calculated.
3. **Payment Reversal:** The refund is initiated to the original form of payment.
4. **E-Ticket Void:** The electronic ticket is voided in the reservation system. A voided ticket cannot be reinstated; the passenger must make a new booking if they wish to travel.
5. **Loyalty Points Adjustment:** If SkyMiles points were earned on the original booking, the points are deducted from the member's account. If the original booking was an award ticket, points are credited back per Section 10.
6. **Confirmation Email:** A cancellation confirmation with refund details (amount, method, estimated timeline) is sent to the passenger.

### 15.3 Deadline Enforcement

Change and cancellation deadlines are enforced by the SKY-RES system based on the scheduled departure time as recorded in the SkyItalia operations database. If a flight departs early (e.g., due to a favourable ATC slot), the originally scheduled time is still used for deadline enforcement. If a flight is delayed, the originally scheduled time is still used; the deadline is not extended by the delay.

This means that if a flight is scheduled at 14:30 and is delayed to 16:00, the 3-hour deadline for Economy Light changes is still 11:30 (3 hours before the original 14:30), not 13:00.

## 16. Multi-Segment and Round-Trip Bookings

### 16.1 Changes to Individual Segments

For bookings containing multiple flight segments (e.g., outbound MXP-CDG and return CDG-MXP), changes can be made to individual segments without affecting the other segments, subject to the following:

- The change fee is applied **per segment** (not per booking).
- Fare differences are calculated on a per-segment basis.
- Changing one segment does not change the fare type of other segments.
- If the change results in an illogical itinerary (e.g., the return flight departs before the new outbound arrives), the system will flag a warning but will not block the change. It is the passenger's responsibility to ensure the itinerary is viable.

### 16.2 Cancellation of Individual Segments

- Economy Light and Economy Standard: Cancelling one segment of a round-trip booking does not affect the other segment. Each segment's cancellation policy applies independently.
- Economy Flex, Premium Economy, and Business: Partial cancellation is permitted and the refund is calculated on a per-segment basis.
- Airport taxes and fees are adjusted on a per-segment basis.

### 16.3 Connecting Itineraries

For itineraries with a connection (e.g., MXP-FCO-CDG booked as a single ticket with a connection in Rome):
- Changes to the connection point are treated as a route change and are subject to route change fees per fare type.
- If the connection time falls below the Minimum Connection Time (MCT) due to a schedule change on one segment, SkyItalia will automatically adjust the itinerary.
- The MCT at FCO is 60 minutes for domestic-to-domestic, 90 minutes for domestic-to-international, and 90 minutes for international-to-international connections.
- At MXP, the MCT is 60 minutes for all connection types (single terminal for SkyItalia operations).

## 17. Special Passenger Categories

### 17.1 Unaccompanied Minors (UM)

Bookings for unaccompanied minors are subject to additional restrictions on changes and cancellations:
- All changes must be made at least **48 hours** before departure (regardless of fare type deadline) to allow ground handling coordination.
- Name changes are not permitted on UM bookings.
- Cancellations follow the standard fare type rules but require the requesting adult to be the same adult who made the original booking (identity verification required).

### 17.2 Passengers Requiring Special Assistance

Passengers who have requested special assistance (wheelchair, stretcher, medical oxygen, etc.) must notify SkyItalia of any booking changes at least **48 hours** before the new departure time to ensure that the necessary ground and cabin arrangements can be made. Standard change fees apply.

### 17.3 Military Personnel

Active-duty military personnel travelling on official orders may be eligible for fee waivers on changes and cancellations. A valid military ID and official travel orders must be presented. Fee waivers are processed at the airport ticket office only (not available online or via the contact centre). This benefit is available for Italian Armed Forces personnel and NATO member country military personnel transiting through Italian airports.

### 17.4 Diplomatic Passport Holders

Passengers travelling on diplomatic or service passports may be eligible for expedited change processing. Standard fees apply unless a bilateral agreement between SkyItalia and the issuing government's transport ministry provides otherwise.

**For reference only — the following rules are no longer in effect but may appear in older booking confirmations or correspondence:**

- **Pre-2023 Policy:** Prior to July 2023, the 24-hour free cancellation rule required flights to be at least 14 days in the future (reduced to 7 days on 1 July 2023).
- **COVID-19 Temporary Measures (2020-2022):** During the COVID-19 pandemic, SkyItalia waived all change fees and offered unlimited free rebooking for all fare types. This temporary measure expired on 31 March 2022. Bookings made during this period that were not yet used retain the pandemic-era flexibility until their travel date.
- **Pre-2025 Refund Timelines:** Prior to 1 March 2025, refund processing times were longer (up to 30 business days for credit card refunds). The current accelerated timelines took effect on 1 March 2025 following system upgrades.
