# SkyItalia Airlines — SkyMiles Loyalty Program

## 1. Program Overview

SkyMiles is the frequent flyer program of SkyItalia Airlines, designed to reward loyal customers with points, tier-based privileges, and access to an extensive network of airline and non-airline partners. Membership is free and open to anyone aged 2 or older. SkyMiles was launched in 2011 as a successor to SkyItalia's earlier "Mille Cieli" program, and has since grown to over 2.8 million registered members across Europe.

SkyMiles operates on a dual-currency model: members earn **Qualifying Points (QP)** that determine tier status, and **Reward Points (RP)** that can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, and partner benefits. In most cases, earning activities generate both QP and RP simultaneously and in equal amounts, but certain promotional or partner-based earnings may generate only Reward Points without contributing to tier qualification. Throughout this document, unless otherwise specified, "points" refers to both QP and RP being earned at the stated rate.

The SkyMiles program year runs from 1 January to 31 December. Tier status earned in a given year becomes effective on 1 February of the following year and remains valid through 31 January of the year after that, giving members a full 12-month enjoyment window regardless of when they qualified.

### 1.1 Program Governance

SkyMiles is governed by the SkyMiles Terms & Conditions, last updated on 15 November 2025. SkyItalia reserves the right to modify program rules, benefits, partner agreements, and earning or redemption rates with 90 days' notice. Members are notified of changes via email and through the SkyMiles section of the SkyItalia website. Italian consumer protection law (Codice del Consumo, D.Lgs. 206/2005) applies to all disputes between Italian-resident members and SkyItalia.

### 1.2 Account Management

Members can manage their SkyMiles account through:
- **skyitalia.com/skymiles** — full account dashboard, point history, tier tracker, redemption catalog
- **SkyItalia mobile app** — digital membership card, boarding pass integration, push notifications for point activity
- **SkyMiles customer service** — phone support with tier-appropriate access levels (see Section 12)

Members must keep their personal details up to date. The name on the SkyMiles account must match the name on the government-issued ID used for travel. Name changes due to marriage, divorce, or legal proceedings require submission of official documentation and are processed within 10 business days.

## 2. Membership Tiers

SkyMiles has four membership tiers. Tier qualification is based on Qualifying Points (QP) earned within a single calendar year.

| Tier | QP Required | Annual Qualification | Minimum Segments (alternative) |
|------|-------------|---------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Blue** (entry level) | 0 | Automatic on enrollment | None |
| **Silver** | 10,000 points | Earn 10K in a calendar year | OR fly 15 qualifying segments |
| **Gold** | 40,000 points | Earn 40K in a calendar year | OR fly 50 qualifying segments |
| **Platinum** | 100,000 points | Earn 100K in a calendar year | OR fly 90 qualifying segments |

**Qualifying segments** are defined as flights operated by SkyItalia or SkyAlliance partner airlines where the member's SkyMiles number is recorded on the ticket at the time of check-in. Codeshare flights earn qualifying segments only when the operating carrier is SkyItalia or a SkyAlliance member. Flights on non-partner airlines, award tickets booked entirely with points, and positioning flights for crew members do not count as qualifying segments.

### 2.1 Tier Retention and Downgrade

Members who do not re-qualify for their current tier in a given year are downgraded by one tier (not to the bottom). For example, a Gold member who earns only 15,000 QP in 2026 would be downgraded to Silver, not Blue, for the 2027 status year. This "soft landing" applies once per downgrade cycle. If the member fails to re-qualify at the Silver level the following year, they would then drop to Blue.

There is a one-time exception for members who are within 10% of their qualification threshold: a Gold member with 36,000+ QP will be granted a 90-day "grace period" into the new year to earn the remaining points. If they succeed, status is maintained without interruption. If not, the soft landing rule applies.

### 2.2 Lifetime Status

Members who have earned 1,000,000 cumulative Qualifying Points over the life of their membership (regardless of annual totals) are awarded **Lifetime Gold** status. Members who have earned 3,000,000 cumulative QP are awarded **Lifetime Platinum** status. Lifetime status holders are exempt from annual re-qualification and retain their tier benefits indefinitely.

## 3. Earning Points on SkyItalia Flights

### 3.1 Base Earning Rates

Points are earned based on the ticket price in EUR (excluding taxes, fees, and surcharges) multiplied by a rate that depends on cabin class:

| Activity | Base Earning Rate |
|----------|------------------|
| Economy flight | **1 point per EUR 1** spent on the ticket |
| Premium Economy flight | **1.5 points per EUR 1** spent |
| Business Class flight | **2 points per EUR 1** spent |

### 3.2 Earning Rates by Fare Type, Cabin Class, and Tier

The following table shows the **complete earning rate per EUR 1 spent** for every combination of fare type, cabin class, and membership tier. The rate includes the base earning plus tier bonus (Silver: +0%, Gold: +25%, Platinum: +50%).

| Fare Type | Cabin Class | Blue | Silver | Gold (+25%) | Platinum (+50%) |
|-----------|------------|------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Economy Light | Economy | 0.5 | 0.5 | 0.625 | 0.75 |
| Economy Standard | Economy | 1.0 | 1.0 | 1.25 | 1.5 |
| Economy Flex | Economy | 1.0 | 1.0 | 1.25 | 1.5 |
| Premium Economy Standard | Premium Economy | 1.5 | 1.5 | 1.875 | 2.25 |
| Premium Economy Flex | Premium Economy | 1.5 | 1.5 | 1.875 | 2.25 |
| Business Saver | Business | 1.5 | 1.5 | 1.875 | 2.25 |
| Business Standard | Business | 2.0 | 2.0 | 2.5 | 3.0 |
| Business Flex | Business | 2.0 | 2.0 | 2.5 | 3.0 |

**Notes:**
- **Economy Light** fares earn at a reduced rate of 0.5 points per EUR because they are the lowest-cost, most restricted fare bucket. Economy Light fares do NOT earn Qualifying Points (QP) — only Reward Points (RP). This means Economy Light tickets contribute to your redemption balance but do not count toward tier qualification.
- **Economy Flex** fares earn at the same base rate as Economy Standard (1.0 pt/EUR) because the fare price already reflects the added flexibility.
- **Business Saver** fares earn at a reduced rate of 1.5 pt/EUR instead of the full Business rate of 2.0 pt/EUR, reflecting the discounted fare cost. Business Saver fares still earn full Qualifying Points.
- The tier bonus percentages (25% for Gold, 50% for Platinum) are applied to the base rate. Silver members earn at the same base rate as Blue members; their benefits are in service rather than earning.
- Points are rounded down to the nearest whole number per transaction.
- For codeshare flights, the earning rate is determined by the **marketing carrier's** fare class mapping, not the operating carrier.

### 3.3 Minimum Point Guarantee

Regardless of the fare paid, members earn a minimum number of points per flight segment:

| Route Type | Minimum Points |
|-----------|---------------|
| Domestic (within Italy) | 250 points |
| Intra-EU / Short-haul (under 1,500 km) | 500 points |
| Medium-haul (1,500 - 3,500 km) | 750 points |
| Long-haul (over 3,500 km) | 1,000 points |

This ensures that even deeply discounted tickets generate a meaningful point earning. The minimum point guarantee applies to all fare types except Economy Light, which receives half the minimum (125/250/375/500 points respectively).

### 3.4 Retroactive Point Crediting

If a member forgets to add their SkyMiles number at the time of booking, points can be credited retroactively by submitting a claim within **6 months** of the flight date. The claim requires: SkyMiles member number, booking reference or e-ticket number, flight date and route, and a copy of the boarding pass or booking confirmation. Claims can be submitted online at skyitalia.com/skymiles/claim or by calling SkyMiles customer service. Processing takes 7-14 business days. Partner airline flights must be claimed within 3 months.

### 5.3 Silver Tier — Detailed Benefits

Silver members receive everything in Blue, plus:

## 6. Redeeming Points

### 6.1 Discount on Flights

- **10,000 points = EUR 100 discount** on any SkyItalia flight.
- Minimum redemption: **5,000 points (EUR 50).**
- Points can be combined with cash payment (partial redemption).
- The discount is applied to the base fare only, not to taxes and fees. For example, if a ticket costs EUR 150 (EUR 110 fare + EUR 40 taxes), a 10,000-point redemption would reduce the fare to EUR 10 + EUR 40 taxes = EUR 50 total.
- There is no maximum redemption limit; the entire fare portion can be covered by points.
- Points-discounted tickets earn Reward Points on the cash portion only (not on the portion covered by points).

## 7. Points Validity and Expiration

- Points **expire after 24 months** of account inactivity.
- Any earning or redemption activity resets the 24-month clock. This includes: flight earnings, partner earnings, credit card earnings, point redemptions, point transfers, and point donations.
- **Gold and Platinum members: points never expire** as long as their tier status is maintained. If a Gold or Platinum member is downgraded to Silver or Blue, the 24-month inactivity clock begins from the date of downgrade.
- Platinum members with Lifetime status: points never expire under any circumstance.
- Members receive an expiration warning email at 18 months of inactivity, 21 months, and 23 months.
- **Preventing expiration:** the easiest way to keep points alive is to earn at least 1 point through any partner activity (e.g., a single Gusto Rewards dining transaction or a credit card purchase). There is no option to pay a fee to extend point validity.
- Points from different earning dates are pooled into a single balance. If points expire due to inactivity, the **entire balance** is forfeited, not just the oldest points.

## 8. Family Pooling — SkyMiles Household

### 8.1 Overview

SkyMiles Household allows family members living at the same address to pool their Reward Points into a shared family balance. This makes it easier for families to accumulate points for award tickets, upgrades, and partner redemptions.

### 8.2 Rules

- **Maximum members:** up to **5 SkyMiles members** can be linked in a single Household account.
- **Same address requirement:** all members must share the same residential address as registered in their SkyMiles profiles. SkyItalia may request proof of address (utility bill, government document) at enrollment or during periodic audits.
- **Account holder:** one member is designated as the Household Account Holder (HAH). The HAH must be 18 years or older and is responsible for managing the pool. The HAH can authorize point transfers between household members and initiate redemptions from the shared pool.
- **Point transfers within Household:** Reward Points can be transferred freely between Household members at no cost, up to 50,000 points per member per calendar year.
- **Qualifying Points:** QP are NOT pooled or transferable. Each member's tier qualification is individual.
- **Tier benefits:** each member retains their own tier status and benefits. A Platinum member's lounge access does not extend to Blue-tier family members unless they are traveling together and the Platinum member exercises their guest privilege.
- **Enrollment:** apply online at skyitalia.com/skymiles/household or at any SkyItalia ticket office. Processing takes 5-10 business days. There is a EUR 20 one-time setup fee per Household.
- **Leaving the Household:** a member can leave a Household at any time, but any points they contributed to the pool remain in the pool. The exiting member starts fresh with zero pooled points (their individually earned points since joining are retained). A member who leaves cannot join a new Household for 6 months.
- **Dissolution:** if the HAH closes the Household, remaining pooled points are distributed equally among all members.

## 9. Status Match and Elite Challenge Program

### 9.1 Status Match

SkyItalia offers a **Status Match** program for frequent flyers who hold elite status with competing airlines. The program allows qualified applicants to receive temporary SkyMiles elite status matching their existing status with another carrier.

**Eligibility:**
- Must hold active elite status (equivalent to Silver or above) with a non-SkyAlliance airline.
- Must not have previously participated in a SkyMiles Status Match within the last 24 months.
- Must provide proof of current status: a screenshot or scan of the airline's loyalty account page showing name, tier, and expiration date.

**Matching table:**

| Other Airline Tier | Matched SkyMiles Tier |
|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Basic Elite / Silver equivalent | Silver (temporary) |
| Mid Elite / Gold equivalent | Gold (temporary) |
| Top Elite / Platinum equivalent | Gold (temporary)* |

*Note: Status Match does not grant Platinum status directly. The highest match is to Gold, with an invitation to the Elite Challenge (see 9.2) to earn Platinum.

**Temporary status duration:** 90 days from the date of activation. After 90 days, the member must have earned enough Qualifying Points to retain the matched tier for the remainder of the program year; otherwise, status reverts to the level supported by their actual QP balance.

**Application:** submit via skyitalia.com/skymiles/statusmatch. Processing takes 5-7 business days. Only one Status Match per member per lifetime.

### 9.2 Elite Challenge

The **Elite Challenge** is an accelerated tier qualification program open to:
- Status Match recipients who wish to earn a higher tier
- New SkyMiles members who anticipate high travel volume
- Existing members who have recently changed their travel patterns

**How it works:**
1. The member applies for the Elite Challenge and selects a target tier (Silver, Gold, or Platinum).
2. SkyItalia provides a reduced QP threshold that must be met within **90 days**:
   - Silver Challenge: earn 5,000 QP in 90 days (vs. standard 10,000 in a year)
   - Gold Challenge: earn 20,000 QP in 90 days (vs. standard 40,000 in a year)
   - Platinum Challenge: earn 50,000 QP in 90 days (vs. standard 100,000 in a year)
3. If the member meets the threshold within the 90-day window, they are awarded the target tier for the remainder of the current program year plus the next full year.
4. If the member does not meet the threshold, they retain whatever tier their QP balance supports under standard rules. There is no penalty for failing the challenge.

**Restrictions:**
- Only one Elite Challenge per member per 12-month period.
- QP earned during the challenge count toward the annual total as well.
- The challenge can be combined with a Status Match (match to Gold, then challenge to Platinum).
- Challenge enrollment fee: EUR 49 (non-refundable).

## 10. Special Programs and Promotions

### 10.1 SkyMiles for Business

SkyItalia offers a **SkyMiles for Business** program that allows companies to earn points on employee travel. The company designates a SkyMiles for Business administrator who manages the corporate account. Points earned by employees on business travel are split: 100% of points go to the employee's personal SkyMiles account for tier qualification, and the company earns a separate pool of Corporate Reward Points (CRP) at a rate of 1 CRP per EUR 5 spent on employee tickets. CRP can be redeemed for business travel, meeting room access at StarStay hotels, or employee reward gift cards.

### 10.2 SkyMiles Credit (Point Purchases)

Members can purchase additional Reward Points to top up their balance:
- **Price:** EUR 25 per 1,000 points
- **Maximum purchase:** 50,000 points per calendar year per member
- **Restrictions:** purchased points are Reward Points only, do not count toward tier qualification, and are credited within 24 hours of purchase
- **Promotional pricing:** during seasonal promotions (typically 2-3 times per year), points may be offered at a 30-50% discount

### 10.3 Point Transfers Between Members

Members can transfer Reward Points to other SkyMiles members (outside of the Household program) for a fee:
- **Transfer fee:** EUR 15 per transaction + EUR 0.01 per point transferred
- **Minimum transfer:** 1,000 points
- **Maximum transfer:** 25,000 points per transaction, 100,000 points per calendar year
- **Processing time:** immediate
- Transferred points are Reward Points only

### 10.4 Point Donations

SkyMiles members can donate points to charity through the SkyMiles for Good program. Partner charities include UNICEF Italy, Italian Red Cross, and Save the Children. Donations of 1,000+ points are tax-deductible in Italy. Points donated are Reward Points only.

## 15. Frequently Asked Questions — SkyMiles

**Q: Can I earn points on codeshare flights?**
A: Yes, but only when the operating carrier is SkyItalia or a SkyAlliance partner. The earning rate is based on the marketing carrier's fare class mapping. Ensure your SkyMiles number is on the booking before travel.

**Q: What happens to my points if I'm downgraded?**
A: Your Reward Points remain in your account. However, if you are downgraded from Gold or Platinum to Silver or Blue, the 24-month inactivity expiration clock begins. Make sure to earn or redeem at least some points within 24 months to keep your balance alive.

**Q: Can I use points to book flights for someone else?**
A: Yes. You can redeem your Reward Points for an award ticket in any name. The person does not need to be a SkyMiles member. However, the booking must be made through your SkyMiles account, and the ticket will show your member number as the point source.

**Q: Are there blackout dates for award tickets?**
A: SkyItalia does not impose calendar blackout dates for award travel. However, award seat availability is limited, and popular routes during peak travel periods (summer, Christmas/New Year, Easter) may have reduced award inventory. Booking 60+ days in advance significantly improves availability.

**Q: How do I check my points balance?**
A: Log in at skyitalia.com/skymiles, use the SkyItalia mobile app, or call the SkyMiles customer service line for your tier. Your balance is updated in real-time for SkyItalia flights and within 14-21 days for partner activities.

**Q: Can I convert SkyMiles points to other loyalty programs?**
A: Currently, point conversion to external programs is not available. Points may be transferred to other SkyMiles members (with fees) or pooled within a SkyMiles Household. SkyItalia periodically evaluates conversion partnerships and any changes will be announced to members.
